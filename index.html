About

Capital Finance Club is a student-led organization at Knox Grammar School focused on corporate finance and asset management. Our curriculum is adapted from NYU Stern MBA Corporate Finance (Spring 2025), providing a structured academic foundation for secondary students.

Our flagship program is the Knox Corporate Finance Challenge: a six-week applied competition in which students analyze real ASX-listed companies and construct defensible investment recommendations based on rigorous valuation and capital structure logic.
